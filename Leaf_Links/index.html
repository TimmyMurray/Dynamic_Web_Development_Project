Leaf Links Golf Club, Co.Galway

Leaf Links Golf club is a new golf club established in Galway in 2015 was designed by leading world designers as a major championship course of international standard. The course is rapidly maturing into one of the best inland courses in Ireland . A parkland course of 6763 yards with fairways of natural meadow grass planted on either side with Spruce, Evergreens and a wide variety of deciduous trees.
